Why this project?

When a disaster happens -- like the recent Turkey-Syria earthquake -- disaster response organisations step in. To inform their planning and resource allocation, they might look to what people are messaging them, either directly, or via social media.


The problem with this, is that there could be hundreds of thousands of relevant messages (if not more). And just as many irrelevant ones. How can a disaster response organisation filter the signal from the noise?


The aim of this project is to build a prototype app that classifies messages as one of 35 pre-labelled categories -- ranging from "Electricity", to "Water", to "Medical products" -- and displays it on this front-end.
